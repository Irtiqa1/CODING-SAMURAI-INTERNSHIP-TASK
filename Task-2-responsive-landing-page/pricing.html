Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our pricing and plans.

Can I change plans anytime?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any differences in billing.
Is there a free trial?
Absolutely! All paid plans include a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans.
Do you offer discounts for nonprofits?
Yes, we offer a 50% discount on all plans for registered nonprofit organizations. Contact our sales team for verification.
