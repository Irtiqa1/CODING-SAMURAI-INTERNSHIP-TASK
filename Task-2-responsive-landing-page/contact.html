Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about getting started with NexaFlow.

How long does it take to set up NexaFlow?
Most teams can get up and running with NexaFlow in under 30 minutes. Our intuitive interface and guided setup make it easy to import your existing projects and invite team members.
Do you offer custom pricing for large teams?
Yes! We offer custom Enterprise plans for teams with 50+ members. These include dedicated account management, custom onboarding, and tailored feature sets. Contact our sales team for a custom quote.
What kind of support do you provide?
All plans include email support with a 24-hour response time. Professional and Enterprise plans include priority support, live chat, and phone support. Enterprise customers also get a dedicated customer success manager.
Can I import data from other project management tools?
Absolutely! We provide import tools for popular platforms like Trello, Asana, Jira, and Monday.com. Our support team can also help with custom migration scenarios for larger implementations.
